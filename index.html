A little more details
about the products

He began his journey into product design with the easy-traveling project, started at the hackathon, the service allowed him to choose a weekend trip to any part of Russia for a specified budget. The work on the project consisted of fully thinking through the functionality and visuals from scratch and how the user will interact with the service, what will be a priority for him and what result he will have to get for choosing a trip, interacting with the schedule already during the trip and upon returning home, system of bonuses and feedback. One of the tour operators planned to join the project, but, alas, everything ended at the stage of discussing mutual integration, and the team decided to take a break in development.

The Venrok sports app ( apps.apple.com/il/app/venrok-workouts/id1479561440) was the first serious project in terms of length and complexity. Joined the development team after they released the first release of the application, created purely by their forces, then they realized the lack of a designer in the team. Venrok positions itself as a sports app for professional athletes and, unlike competitors, allows you to customize your workout in detail in the most detailed details. To the basic interface, which included a list of workouts and exercises, and a basic player, onboards were added, advanced registration, a subscription model, functional customization for each exercise, constants, synchronization with google fit and apple health, profile extension, and application customization, bundles and programs, with the ability to create them for coaches, a feed and a user tracking system, the creation of a community in a sports area of ​​interest, advanced statistics with the display of many parameters, including a visual display of the muscles involved in the training process. In addition, he was engaged in the design of the page for the stores, updating the identity, thinking through the creatives for advertising campaigns, researching with real people to identify gaps in the user experience and improving the interface. At the moment, this project is already about two years old and work on it continues.

The next project was the Lifetables habit tracker ( apps.apple.com/us/app/lifetables-motivation-habits/id1527590620). People are interested in observing the statistics of their lives and mentally relaxing at the sight of what path they have taken, therefore, together with one of the developers of the previous project, a new application was created. Unlike Venrok, my work on this project was carried out from the very beginning, and I went from drawing a user flow in a notebook and conducting tests on real people with prototypes to the first release and then adding and fixing functions. The work on the project continues now.
